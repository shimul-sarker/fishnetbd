Our commitments

Seafood with a conscience.

From mangrove restoration in the Sundarbans to fair-wage farmer cooperatives — sustainability is built into every decision we make.

ASC-certified sourcing

100% of our shrimp comes from ASC or BAP-certified farms with verified feed conversion ratios.

Mangrove restoration

Since 2018 we have funded the planting of 1.2 million mangrove seedlings in the Sundarbans.

Carbon-aware logistics

Slow-steaming reefers, route optimisation and shore-power at Rotterdam reduce kg CO₂e per kg shipped.

Fair-wage farmers

Our 4,200 farmer partners earn a guaranteed floor price reviewed twice a year.

Zero-waste plants

Shells and trim are converted to chitosan, fertiliser and pet-food feedstock — landfill rate <0.5%.

Bycatch reduction

Vessel partners use turtle-excluder devices and circle hooks to keep bycatch below 3%.
