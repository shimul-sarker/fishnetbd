Our story

Sixteen years of honest seafood.

From a single cold-store in Chattogram to a vertically-integrated exporter shipping to 27 EU countries — Fishnet was built on one belief: premium quality is non-negotiable.

Milestones

Sixteen years in motion

2009

Founded in Chattogram

Fishnet Seafoods opens its first BRC-track processing line on the Karnaphuli river.

2012

First EU container

Inaugural reefer shipment of black tiger shrimp lands in Rotterdam.

2015

BRC Issue 7 certified

Achieves AA-grade on the Global Standard for Food Safety.

2018

ASC chain-of-custody

Partners with 14 ASC-certified farms across the Sundarbans delta.

2021

Rotterdam office

European desk opens for direct sales, customs and customer support.

2024

Value-added plant

New breaded & marinated line commissioned for private-label retail.

Mission

To bring honest, traceable seafood from the Bay of Bengal to every European kitchen.

Vision

To be the most trusted seafood partner in Europe — measured by quality, traceability and the prosperity of our coastal communities.
