Logistics process

From catch to your cold-store

01

Harvest & reception

Daily collection from 220 farm sites and three landing ports along the Karnaphuli river.

02

Processing & IQF

Within six hours of harvest, product is graded, processed and frozen at -35°C.

03

Reefer stuffing

Loaded into pre-cooled 40-ft reefers at -22°C at our on-site container yard.

04

Ocean transit

22–24 day transit via Colombo to Rotterdam, Antwerp, Hamburg or Felixstowe.

05

EU clearance

Our Rotterdam desk handles BIP inspection, customs and last-mile distribution.

06

Delivery

Refrigerated truck to your DC anywhere in the EU within 72 hours of port release.
