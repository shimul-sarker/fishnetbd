Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, certifications, logistics and payment.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our standard minimum is one 40-ft reefer container (approx. 24 tons). Mixed-product containers are possible for established partners.

Which certifications do you hold?

HACCP, BRC Issue 9 (AA), ISO 22000, ASC chain-of-custody, MSC for selected wild species and EU-approved establishment numbers for all three facilities.

How long is the transit time to Europe?

Typically 22–24 days from Chattogram to Rotterdam, Antwerp or Hamburg via Colombo trans-shipment.

Do you offer private-label / OEM production?

Yes. We produce under more than 30 European retail labels including artwork, multilingual EU labelling and nutritional declaration.

What payment terms are available?

New customers: 30% TT advance, 70% on B/L copy. Established partners: open account with credit insurance.

How can I receive samples?

Air-freight samples (up to 5 kg) can be dispatched within 48 hours of inquiry. Request via our contact form.

Can you supply during religious / holiday periods?

Yes — our production runs year-round and we keep four weeks of finished stock to cover Eid, Ramadan and Christmas demand peaks.
