How we work

From coastal water to your warehouse.

A transparent four-step process designed for European importers who value precision.

01 / Sourcing

Selective harvesting

Our network of 240+ vetted farms and 12 deep-sea vessels supply only catches that meet our size, freshness and sustainability criteria.

02 / Processing

EU-compliant processing

BAP & BRC certified processing facilities with IQF tunnels, automated grading lines and on-site QC labs that test every batch against international standards.

03 / Logistics

Unbroken cold chain

−18 °C reefer containers leave Chittagong weekly. Live temperature data is shared with your team in real time.

04 / Delivery

DDP into your facility

We handle EU customs, veterinary inspection and last-mile delivery — you simply receive perfect product at your dock.
